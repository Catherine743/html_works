Our Story

Since 2018 we strive to bring authentic flavours of the Persian and Mediterranean to the city of Alleppey. Our fresh ingredients and delicate blend of spices allow us to craft bright and mouth watering dishes that are unknown for most of our beloved ones.

Al Jazeel will be a greatest and only destination for them to explore that taste in our home town. This is not our arrogance it's our confidence. We will perspire and persevere at every extent to make that to happen.
