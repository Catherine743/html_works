Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1-2 teaspoons oil
  • About 1 cup warm water,or as needed
  • Dry flour for dusting

How to make it

To make chapati, combine wheat flour and salt,then gradually add water and oil to form a soft dough that you knead until smooth.

After resting the dough, divide it into balls, roll them into thin circles, cook on a hot frypan until both sides have brown spots.
